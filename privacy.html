This privacy policy governs your use of the smart device application 'Distance Measurer' by 'Turkic'.

What information does 'Distance Measurer' obtain and how is it used?

'Distance Measurer' does not collect or transmit any personally identifiable information and as no personal information is collected or transmitted, we have nothing to share with anyone.

'Distance Measurer' requires access to your device camera and image gallery to operate. These are kept on your device only and not shared or transferred unless manually shared by you.

'Distance Measurer' does not use or collect any data related to your geographic location.

What about advertising?

'Distance Measurer' uses a third party product 'AdMob' by 'Google inc.' to serve personalized advertisements. 'AdMob' has access to device advertising id and generates advertisement based on user's interests. You can access 'Google inc.' privacy policy here

Do you use vendors or analytics providers?

No. The Application has no facility for collecting, transmitting, or maintaining user data, so no data is shared with vendors or analytics providers.
